Q&A.

Frequently Asked Questions

When are you releasing?

Ground Operator is aimed to release sometime in 2026.

What is Ground Operator?

Ground Operator is a Roblox game about servicing planes and managing ground crew at various airports. It uses a research based progression system, similar to the likes of KSP. It's multiplayer, so you will be able to play with friends, and possibly collaborate in the future.

How can I play Ground Operator?

You can apply for tester when applications are open, or become a developer and gain access to the game before release.
