About Horison Studios

Horison Studios is a professional Roblox game development studio specializing in realistic simulation experiences. Founded with a passion for aviation and ground operations, we create immersive, high-quality games that push the boundaries of what's possible on the Roblox platform.

Our flagship project, Ground Operator, represents our commitment to delivering authentic, engaging experiences that combine realistic gameplay mechanics with innovative A.I systems and custom-built assets.
